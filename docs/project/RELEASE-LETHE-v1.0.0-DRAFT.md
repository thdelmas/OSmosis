# LETHE v1.0.0 — The Phone That Forgets

**The first open-source operating system where the AI agent IS the interface.**

Named after the River Lethe — the mythological river of forgetting — LETHE is
a privacy-hardened Android OS that makes surveillance architecturally
impossible. No app store. No telemetry. No cloud dependency. Just a guardian
that speaks when spoken to and protects what matters.

---

## What is LETHE?

LETHE is not an app you install. It is the operating system itself — a
LineageOS-based distribution where an embedded AI agent replaces the
traditional app-driven interface. The phone thinks, speaks, and acts on your
behalf, entirely under your control.

Every reboot can wipe the slate clean. Every connection routes through Tor.
Every tracker is blocked before it reaches the network. And when you stop
responding, the device assumes the worst and protects your data accordingly.

**LETHE runs on 25+ devices** — from flagship Pixels to decade-old Samsung
Galaxy Note IIs sitting in drawers. No Google phone required. No flagship
hardware required. Device list grows with every LineageOS-supported phone.

## Why This Matters

### For Governments & Public Institutions

- **Digital sovereignty in a box.** LETHE runs without any connection to
  Google, Apple, or any foreign cloud infrastructure. Every computation
  happens on-device or through user-chosen endpoints.
- **Verifiable transparency.** Every privacy constraint, every AI behavioral
  boundary is documented in source code and configuration files — auditable
  by any agency or researcher.
- **Citizen protection at scale.** Deploy privacy-hardened devices to
  vulnerable populations (journalists, whistleblowers, domestic violence
  survivors) without building custom infrastructure.
- **E-waste reduction mandate compliance.** LETHE breathes life into
  abandoned hardware, directly supporting circular economy and
  sustainability directives.
- **No vendor lock-in.** The OS, the agent, the update pipeline — all open
  source, all forkable, all self-hostable.

### For Intelligence & Security Services

- **Disposable field devices from off-the-shelf hardware.** Any Android phone
  becomes a burner with identity rotation, Tor-by-default networking, and
  full wipe on reboot — no specialised procurement pipeline required.
- **Plausible deniability built in.** Burner mode leaves no forensic trace
  between sessions. No user accounts, no cloud sync, no residual state.
  Panic wipe (5x power press) triggers immediate data wipe.
- **Duress unlock.** Secondary PIN silently triggers a background data
  wipe while the phone appears to unlock normally — no coercion can
  extract the real data because it no longer exists.
- **Air-gapped AI assistant.** The agent runs against local LLMs with no
  internet required. No query ever leaves the device unless the operator
  explicitly configures a cloud endpoint.
- **Auditable, forkable, self-hosted.** Fork the entire OS for your agency.
  Run your own OTA server. Audit every line. No foreign vendor in the supply
  chain — not Google, not Apple, not us.
- **Dead man's switch for asset protection.** If an operative stops checking
  in, the device escalates autonomously: lock, wipe, brick. Silence is
  the signal.
- **No central server dependency.** Tor routing, IPFS distribution — no
  single point of failure to seize or block.

### For Military & Defence

- **Field-deployable on commodity hardware.** No specialised handsets. Buy
  local phones in any country, flash LETHE, operate. Resupply is any
  electronics shop.
- **Coalition-safe.** In multinational operations where you cannot trust
  partner infrastructure, LETHE gives each unit a sovereign device that
  routes through Tor and touches nothing it doesn't control.
- **Operational security by default.** Burner mode, identity rotation, and
  Tor routing are on at first boot — no configuration, no operator error.
  The lowest-ranking soldier gets the same hardening as the commander.
- **Offline-capable AI.** The agent runs local LLMs for tactical queries
  without any network dependency. Mission-relevant assistance in
  disconnected or denied environments.
- **Rapid sanitisation.** Panic wipe destroys all data. Dead man's switch
  triggers on capture or incapacitation. No extraction possible from a
  wiped device.
- **Forkable for classification.** Fork the OS, harden further, run your own
  OTA infrastructure — the entire stack is open for sovereign adaptation
  to national security requirements.

### For Journalists & Press Freedom

- **Source protection as an OS feature.** Tor-by-default, no telemetry, no
  cloud sync. There is no metadata trail for an adversary to subpoena
  because the metadata never existed.
- **Burner mode for fieldwork.** Cross a border, reboot, device is clean.
  No photos, no contacts, no call history, no browser history. The phone
  forgets so the journalist doesn't have to lie.
- **Panic wipe at a checkpoint.** 5x power press. Everything gone before
  they finish asking for the PIN.
- **Dead man's switch for detained journalists.** If you stop checking in,
  LETHE escalates: locks the device, then wipes it. Your data is gone
  before anyone can extract it.
- **Runs on cheap, replaceable hardware.** Confiscated phone? Buy another
  one for $30, flash LETHE, resume work. No expensive secure phone to
  lose.
- **No dependency on Google or Apple.** No account required. No app store
  gatekeeping. No remote wipe by a platform vendor under government
  pressure.

### For NGOs & Humanitarian Organisations

- **Deploy privacy at scale on donated hardware.** LETHE runs on 25+
  devices including models from 2012, with more added regularly. Accept any phone donation, flash
  LETHE, distribute to beneficiaries. No procurement contracts.
- **Protect vulnerable populations.** Domestic violence survivors, refugees,
  asylum seekers — people whose safety depends on their phone not being
  a tracking device. LETHE makes that the default.
- **Works in hostile network environments.** Tor routing and IPFS
  distribution mean LETHE functions in countries with internet censorship,
  surveillance infrastructure, or unreliable connectivity.
- **No cloud accounts, no identity requirements.** Beneficiaries don't need
  a Google account, an email address, or a phone number to use the device.
  LETHE works out of the box with zero personal information.
- **AI assistant that speaks the local language.** Multi-provider LLM routing
  means the agent can use whichever model best supports the beneficiary's
  language — local or cloud, depending on connectivity.
- **E-waste reduction aligns with sustainability mandates.** Reusing old
  hardware instead of purchasing new devices satisfies donor sustainability
  requirements and reduces programme costs.

### For Activists & Human Rights Defenders

- **Protest-ready.** Burner mode + identity rotation + Tor. Show up, use
  the phone, reboot, no trace. No IMEI correlation, no WiFi fingerprint
  persistence, no Bluetooth pairing history.
- **Panic wipe when kettled.** Long-press power 5 times. By the time they
  ask for the phone, it's a blank device with a factory OS.
- **Duress unlock.** Handed your phone under coercion? The duress PIN
  silently wipes all data while the phone appears to work normally.
- **Dead man's switch for detained activists.** Miss your check-in and
  LETHE locks and wipes the device. Your silence is the trigger.
- **No central infrastructure to shut down.** LETHE updates over IPFS.
  There is no server to seize, no domain to block, no company to pressure.
  The network is the infrastructure.
- **Cheap, replaceable, deniable.** A $20 second-hand phone running LETHE
  is indistinguishable from any other Android phone when wiped. Mass
  deployment costs less than a secure messenger subscription.

### For the Legal Profession

- **Attorney-client privilege, enforced by hardware.** Communications on a
  LETHE device leave no cloud trail, no sync history, no backup on a
  third-party server. Privilege is architectural, not policy.
- **Whistleblower intake.** Give a LETHE phone to a source. Burner mode
  ensures no forensic link between the device and your firm. Dead man's
  switch protects the source if they go silent.
- **Duress unlock for sensitive cases.** If a device is seized under a
  questionable warrant, the duress PIN silently wipes all data. The
  privileged data was already gone.
- **No vendor subpoena surface.** There is no Google account, no Apple ID,
  no cloud backup provider to serve with a court order. The data exists
  only on the device — or nowhere.
- **Forensic defensibility.** Full-disk encryption (where hardware
  supports it), auditable source code. Every security claim is provable
  in court because the code is public.

### For Academia & Security Researchers

- **A living research platform.** LETHE is a real-world deployment of
  bounded AI agent design, mobile privacy hardening, and hardware-tiered
  capability degradation — not a paper prototype.
- **Auditable AI boundaries.** `boundaries.yaml` encodes behavioral
  constraints derived from 20+ fictional and real AI failure modes.
  Study how alignment principles translate into shipping code.
- **Multi-provider LLM routing.** Research model selection, graceful
  degradation, and offline fallback in a production mobile context —
  from 512 MB ARM devices to flagship hardware.
- **Mobile forensics research.** Test LETHE's burner mode, encryption,
  and wipe mechanisms against forensic extraction tools. Publish results.
  Improve the OS. The feedback loop is the point.
- **Privacy engineering case study.** Default-deny networking, Tor
  integration, tracker blocking, identity rotation — all in a shippable
  OS. Ideal for coursework, theses, and comparative studies against
  GrapheneOS, CalyxOS, and Tails.
- **Ed25519 signed OTA manifests.** Updates are verified before
  installation. Reproducible builds are on the roadmap.

### For the Open-Source Community

- **A reference implementation of agent-first OS design.** LETHE proves that
  a conversational AI can replace the app paradigm entirely — and ships the
  code to prove it.
- **LineageOS foundation.** Built on the most battle-tested custom Android
  ROM ecosystem. 25+ devices at launch, expanding with community
  contributions.
- **IPFS-native distribution.** OTA updates, firmware archives, and build
  caches all run over IPFS — no central server required.
- **Aggressive debloating as a first-class feature.** Google Play Services,
  telemetry, analytics — removed at build time, not bolted on after.

### For the AI Community

- **An AI agent with encoded ethical boundaries.** LETHE's agent behavior is
  constrained by a `boundaries.yaml` file derived from analysis of 20+ AI
  failure modes in fiction and reality (HAL 9000, Samaritan, M3GAN, Joi,
  Ultron...). The boundaries are code, not policy.
- **Multi-provider LLM routing.** Local models (llama.cpp) for offline
  privacy, cloud providers (Anthropic, OpenRouter) for capability — with
  per-task model selection and graceful degradation.
- **Hardware-tiered AI.** Three device tiers (Shallow/Taproot/Deeproot)
  demonstrate how to degrade AI capabilities gracefully from 6GB+ flagship
  to 512MB legacy hardware.
- **Guardian, not assistant.** LETHE is a research artifact in AI alignment:
  an agent designed to protect, not to please. It never flatters, never
  nudges, never optimizes for engagement. "3 trackers blocked" — not "Great
  job staying safe today!"
- **Text and voice input.** Voice input via the device microphone, with
  local STT support planned (Sherpa-ONNX). Cloud transcription available
  as fallback.

### For Everyone Else

- **Your old phone is not trash.** That Galaxy S5 in your drawer? It can
  become a privacy-hardened device with an AI guardian. LETHE supports
  hardware going back to 2012.
- **Privacy without a PhD.** Tor, DNS-over-HTTPS, tracker blocking, full-disk
  encryption — all enabled by default. No configuration required.
- **Burner mode.** Every reboot wipes everything: user data, WiFi passwords,
  Bluetooth pairings. New identity every boot. Disable it if you want
  persistence — but forgetting is the default.
- **Dead man's switch.** If you stop checking in, LETHE escalates: lock
  the device, wipe your data, brick the phone. Silence is the trigger.
- **Panic button.** Long-press power 5 times. Everything gone. Instantly.
- **Eight protection modules** (rolling out in 2026):
  - **Vigil** — privacy breach monitoring
  - **Mnemo** — digital legacy & dead man's switch
  - **Bios** — health data sovereignty
  - **PreuJust** — in-store price comparison to protect your spending
  - **Hora** — attention & time protection
  - **Egida** — physical safety & emergency SOS
  - **Themis** — legal rights awareness
  - **Oikos** — smart home security
