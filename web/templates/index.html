What kind of device?

Different devices need different tools. Pick the category that matches your device.

📱

Phone or tablet

Samsung, Google Pixel, Xiaomi, Fairphone, PinePhone, and other mobile devices.

💻

Computer or SBC

Bootable USB/SD for PCs, Raspberry Pi, Jetson, and single-board computers.

🖧

Router or NAS

Flash OpenWrt on routers, PXE boot servers, or network storage devices.

🚗

Car or vehicle

Android Auto head units, Raspberry Pi dashboards, and OBD2 devices.

⛵

Boat or marine

Chart plotters, NMEA 2000 gateways, SignalK hubs, and marine electronics.

📡

IoT or wearable

ESP32, smart home devices, IP cameras, PineTime, and other embedded hardware.

🎮

Game console or media

Nintendo Switch, Steam Deck, Chromecast, Fire TV, Kindle, and similar devices.

📍

GPS or navigation

Garmin, TomTom, drone controllers, and standalone navigation devices.
